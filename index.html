Discover Mathilde Ma Muse

Mathilde Simon is the creator of her brand, "Mathilde my muse."A former ceramist, it was while practicing her craft that she discovered stones and their powers.It is the myth of a designer who started with ceramic amulets, often with children's faces.At the birth of these children and nephews, Mathilde feels the need to transmit these emotions and her creativity through her profession.

"To create to live" it makes sense. It is then that Mathilde creates a new world for herself in her workshop.A peaceful place, conducive to creation and imagination. When you enter his shop you will be pleasantly surprised by the smells of violets that will take you back to your childhood.
